About

What Sportify does, in plain terms.

Sportify (TURF MAX) is a full-stack sports booking and match-management platform built around turf and ground reservations. It lets players find and book turfs, form teams, get matched against other teams, join tournaments, and keep a running history of their matches. Under the hood it's a TypeScript monorepo with a React client, a Node/Express API, and a PostgreSQL database managed through Drizzle ORM — with an unusually thorough set of setup, schema, and audit docs alongside the code.

Features

Core capabilities across booking, teams, and competition.

Turf discovery & booking

Browse turf listings, filter by preference, view details, and book with automatic conflict checking.

🧑‍🤝‍🧑

Create teams and manage membership so groups can play and compete together.

⚔️

Create matches, send invitations, and keep a full match history over time.

List and register for tournaments alongside regular match play.

Admin-facing views into users and system data, backed by a documented audit trail.
